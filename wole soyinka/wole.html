PROFESSOR WOLE SOYINKA

The nigerain playwriter

Here's a time line of Professor Wole Soyinka:

  • 1934 - Born in ABEOKUTA, NIGERIA
  • 1952 -He attend the Univeristy of ibadan for two years(1952-1954)
  • 1957 - He completed his undergraduate education at the University of Leeds in England.Soyinka received his first degree in drama at Leeds under the instruction of world-renowned Shakespearean critic G. Wilson Knight.He worked briefly as a play reader at the Royal Court Theatre in London before returning to Nigeria to study Africa drama.
  • 1975 - Soyinka taught at the University of Lagos,University of Ibadan and the University of Ife(now call OBAFEMI AWOLOWO UNIVERSITY) in Ile-Ife, Nigeria. At the faculty the latter instituition since 1975,he is currently a Professor of comparative literature.
  • 1984 -He had one of his play titled "The Man" banned by a Nigeria court .
  • 1986 -Soyinka won the Nobel Prize in Literature.
  • 1994 - In November, Soyinka fled Nigeria to brief exile in the United States.
  • 1999 -Soyinka return in NIgeriua when civil rule was restored in Nigeria.
